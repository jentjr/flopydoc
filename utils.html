flopy.export.utils Module¶

flopy.export.utils.ensemble_helper(inputs_filename, outputs_filename, models, add_reals=True, **kwargs)¶

helper to export an ensemble of model instances. Assumes all models have same dis and sr, only difference is properties and boundary conditions. Assumes model.nam.split(‘_’)[-1] is the realization suffix to use in the netcdf variable names
